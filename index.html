About Me

I received the B.Sc. and M.Sc degrees in Electrical Engineering from the University of Kurdistan, Sanandaj, in 2008, and the Shahid Rajaee Teacher Training University, Tehran, in 2013, respectively. I started my Ph.D. at the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro in August 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Rodrigo C. de Lamare and Prof. Vítor H. Nascimento. I am currently working on energy-efficient techniques applied to adaptive IoT networks and federated learning.

Research Interests

Statistical Signal Processing, Distributed Optimization, Federated Learning, Deep Learning

Applications

Adaptive Networks, Internet of Things, Wireless Sensor Networks
